Valencian Community

Alicante

The campus of the University of Alicante lies in San Vicente del Raspeig, bordering the city of Alicante to the north. More than 25,000 students attend the University.

Valencia

Valencia is the capital of the autonomous community of Valencia and the third-largest city in Spain after Madrid and Barcelona, surpassing 800,000 inhabitants in the municipality.

Castellón

The city is notorious for its music festivals, among which we find: the Tanned Tin music festival for alternative music, the Benicàssim's International Festival, the Arenal Sound and the Rototom Sunsplash Festival, known for its reggae music.

Benidorm

Benidorm es una ciudad y municipio español de la provincia de Alicante, en la Comunidad Valenciana. Está situada a orillas del mar Mediterráneo, en la comarca de la Marina Baja, de la que es su municipio más denso y poblado con una densidad de 1792 hab/km² y 69 738 habitantes

Játiva

Játiva is a municipality and a city in the Valencian Community located in the south of the province of Valencia, in the north of the Central Regions and capital of the La Costera region. It has the largest number of enclaves in all of Spain, with a total of twenty-six, and in 2020 it had 29,623 inhabitants.

Elche

Elche is a city and municipality in Spain located in the province of Alicante, in the Valencian Community. Capital of Bajo Vinalopó, the municipality has 235,580 inhabitants, with the population of its urban area being 191,429 inhabitants.
